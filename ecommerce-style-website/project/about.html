Our mission

Built for the work, not around it.

Session Supply Co. exists because preparation time is a real constraint for most facilitators. Between caseloads, coordination, and the emotional demands of peer recovery work, creating session materials from scratch isn't sustainable.

We build structured, ready-to-use resources that give facilitators a strong foundation — so they can spend their energy where it matters most: with the people they serve.

Every resource is developed in collaboration with practicing peer recovery specialists, reviewed for alignment with evidence-based principles, and tested in real group settings before publication.

"When the plan is solid, the facilitator can be fully present. That's what we're trying to make possible."

What we stand for

Four principles behind every resource.

Clarity

Every plan has a clear structure: opening, body, close. No ambiguity about what happens next or what the facilitator should do.

Intentionality

Nothing is in a session plan by accident. Every prompt, every pause, every transition is purposeful and explained in facilitator notes.

Trust

We write for practitioners who know what they're doing. No condescension, no over-explanation — just clear tools for skilled people.

Humanity

Peer recovery is profoundly human work. Our language reflects that — warm, grounded, and respectful of both facilitator and participant.

How we work

How every resource gets made.

We don't write session plans in a vacuum. Each resource goes through a development process grounded in real facilitation practice.

Browse resources
01

Practitioner input

Every resource begins with conversations with active peer recovery specialists. We ask about their real challenges, their group dynamics, what's missing from existing tools.

02

Evidence-based structure

We draw on Motivational Interviewing, Trauma-Informed Care, and Strengths-Based facilitation frameworks. Session structures are reviewed for alignment with SAMHSA guidelines.

03

Field testing

Resources are tested in live group settings before publication. Facilitators report back on timing, language clarity, and participant response. We revise based on what we learn.

04

Continuous updates

Purchasers receive free updates as resources are revised. If you bought it, you own the current best version — always.

The story

Started in the work, built for the work.

Session Supply Co. grew out of a simple observation: the most effective facilitators weren't necessarily the ones who prepared the most — they were the ones whose preparation freed them to be fully present.

We built the tools we wished existed. Clear, structured, practical resources that give facilitators a solid floor to stand on — so they can focus on the room, not the plan.

Today we serve peer recovery specialists, program coordinators, and community health facilitators across the country. The work remains the same: clear tools, calm design, and total respect for the people doing this work.
